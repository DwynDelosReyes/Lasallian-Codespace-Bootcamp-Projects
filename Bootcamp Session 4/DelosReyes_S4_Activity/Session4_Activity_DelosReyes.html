About International Computer Company

At the heart of innovation and technological excellence stands the International Computer Company (ICC). With a history spanning over three decades, ICC has consistently pushed the boundaries of technology and computing. Our journey began in a modest garage, where a group of visionary tech enthusiasts shared a dream to revolutionize the digital landscape. Since then, ICC has evolved into a global tech powerhouse, leading the charge in cutting-edge solutions and groundbreaking innovations. Headquartered in the vibrant tech hub of Silicon Valley, our commitment to excellence and diversity has enabled us to attract top talents from around the world.
